Hit the gap!

Aug 30, 2019
games hit the gap inspiration

The inspiration of hit the gap originates from years ago, when I was just a little kid. Back before when your imagination was a much larger part of your daily entertainment.

Having nothing to do in the car, I’d try and judge when my car would line up in between things, cars, trees, road lights, that kind of thing, and when I thought I was right in between I’d click my teeth together. It sounds silly, and I guess it is silly, but that simple little game in my head got a lot of usage out of me.

So when I decided I wanted to try building a game I thought why not keep it simple? And there you see the inspiration for the silly name I’ve given myself.
